Biography

I'm a PhD Student in the BIO-MAC Laboratory, Department of Chemistry with Dr. A. Sreekanth at the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NITT) where I Have Been Awarded the Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF). Prior to Which I Spent A Year as an Assisstat Professor in the Department of Chemistry, Guruvdev Arts and Science College, Kannur University. I Obtained MSc from National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NITT) in 2017, and BSc from Payyannur College, Kannur University in 2015, all in Chemistry. My Broad Area Of Research Is Bioinorganic Chemistry. I'm Trying To Develop Thiosemicarbazone Derivatives And Metal Complexes For In-Silico/In-Vitro Biological Applications, Especially For Anticancer Studies. I Aim To Study The Structure-Activity Relationship Of Thiosemicarbazones And Their Metal Complexes.
